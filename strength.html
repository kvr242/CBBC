STRENGTH AND CONDITIONING

Why Strength and Conditioning?

I learned first hand the impact strength and conditioning can have on my overall game. After dedicating 2 off-seasons to getting stronger, more flexible and explosive I was able to gain 20 lbs of muscle. This translated directly to my overall game.


My senior year I saw exactly HOW strength and conditioning directly impacted my game as I...
-Increased my Vertical by OVER 10 inches.
-Saw my scoring average increase from 11 PPG to 19.2 PPG.
-Increased Rebounding average from less than 4 to 8.2 RPG.
-Increased my 3 point %.
-Increased my overall FG%.

FAQs


Will lifting impact my shot?
Not if you're lifting appropriately. Each program is designed to help maximize potential.
The programs not only will HELP overall jumpshot, but will help improve jumpshot by incorporating balancing exercises, explosive movements and developing a strong core and legs.
What if I've never lifted weight before?
Initial movements will have to be learned.
For YOUNGER athletes, the workouts will be less weights and more bodyweight exercises.
For High School, College and Pro there will be more weights.Movements will have to be mastered prior to adding weight to ensure injury prevention and maximizing workload potential.
