How the program works

STEP 1

Free Consultation

-A brief 15-20 minute workout will be performed to assess focus of program focusing on BENCHMARK drills.
-Based of initial assessment, conversation will take place immediately after to discuss focus points.
-If agreement to move forward with program, then we move on to step 2.
- Workout for Day 1 will be emailed the day before 1st workout.

STEP 2

Day 1 of Program

-1st 10 minutes of Program will consist of VIDEO RECORDING of shooting from determined approaches.
-The recording will serve multiple purposes.

1.) To visualize starting form and monitor improvements through duration of program.
2.) To further determine and develop complete program for Shooting Academy for athlete.

-Complete first workout.
Few days after workout, summary of workout focus for the program will be discussed.

STEP 3

Completion of Program

-Athlete will be re-tested based off initial assessment drills and re-recorded.
-After completion of final workout, athlete will be scheduled follow up call where they will be sent summary of program,
addressing areas of focus, areas to focus on AFTER program and comparison of initial video to final video.
