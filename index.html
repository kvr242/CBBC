Corey Hassan Basketball

Corey Hassan Sports has one goal.To provide athletes with the best possible instruction and guidance to help them accomplish their goals. We will look to provide the best trainings, clinics, camps, leagues, teams, tournaments and resources available.


A HUGE differentiator between us and other available options is that it is a Requirement that every single one of our trainers for our skill development trainings MUST have either played or coached at the collegiate or professional level.


Our goal is to provide much more than just the appropriate training from those who actually practiced what they preached, but also inspiration for each and every athlete to know the trainer working with them has been there and so can they! Our trainers aim to be role models and mentors for those athletes we work with.
